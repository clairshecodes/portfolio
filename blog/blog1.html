A Happy Place

School Project - Affective Computing, Jan 24, 2020

Chronic illnesses affect all dimensions of human life and can take it's toll on a person's emotional well-being. Being a child and hospitilized places additional burden to the child's psychology. However, children's vivid imagination allows for their minds to be easily distracted from issues with simple activities such as playing games. Our work is based on existing work based on guided imagery, combined with different aspects of play. In this way to help children overcome the emotional effects of chronic illness.
