Health application

The health application is based on a user-and doctor UI. There is an app for the patient where the patient can log in data like activity, heart palpitation and time. While the doctor can read the patients data and simply get an overview of all the data the patient logged in. Furthemore the doctor and the patient can see the heart rate of the patient. This project was made in school with a group.
